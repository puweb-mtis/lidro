LIDRO Droid BaseBoard

Baseboard
Front
LIDRO base board is equipped with self-developed lidar sensor and developed by grafting multi-sensor control and convergence technology. It can be modified to fit the purpose on top of the baseboard and can be used for multiple purposes. In particular, it can be used for multiple purposes by incorporating all three self-developed lidar sensors.
